# Onboarding Interview Template

This document contains the interview questions used to gather universal context for the project.

## Project Basics

1. **Project Name**: What is the name of this project?
2. **Project Type**: What type of project is this? (e.g., web app, CLI tool, library, API)
3. **Primary Purpose**: What is the main problem this project solves?

## Goals and Objectives

4. **Primary Goals**: What are the top 3-5 primary goals for this project?
5. **Success Metrics**: How will you measure success?
6. **Target Users**: Who is the intended audience or user base?

## Technical Context

7. **Technology Stack**: What technologies, languages, frameworks will be used?
8. **Architecture**: What is the high-level architecture or system design?
9. **Dependencies**: What external services, APIs, or dependencies are required?
10. **Platform Requirements**: What platforms must this support? (OS, browsers, devices)

## Constraints and Limitations

11. **Time Constraints**: What are the key deadlines or timeline requirements?
12. **Resource Constraints**: What are budget, team size, or resource limitations?
13. **Technical Constraints**: Are there any technical limitations or requirements?
14. **Compliance Requirements**: Any regulatory, security, or compliance needs?

## Team and Process

15. **Team Structure**: Who is on the team and what are their roles?
16. **Communication**: How does the team communicate and collaborate?
17. **Development Process**: What development methodology is used? (Agile, Waterfall, etc.)
18. **Tools and Workflows**: What tools are used for development, testing, deployment?

## Documentation and Knowledge

19. **Existing Documentation**: What documentation already exists?
20. **Knowledge Sources**: Where can agents find project knowledge? (docs, wikis, code comments)
21. **Decision Log**: How are important decisions documented?

## Future Considerations

22. **Planned Features**: What features are planned for future releases?
23. **Scalability**: What are the expected scale requirements?
24. **Maintenance**: What are the long-term maintenance expectations?

## Additional Context

25. **Special Requirements**: Any other important context, requirements, or considerations?

---

**Usage**: This template is used by the interview script to gather information. Agents should reference the compiled `docs/universal_context.md` for the actual project context.
