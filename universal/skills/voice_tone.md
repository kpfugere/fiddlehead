# Voice and Tone Skill

Maintain consistent voice and tone throughout the document.

## Purpose

Ensure the writing maintains the desired voice and tone as defined in `universal/02_voice_tone_style.md`.

## Process

1. Review `universal/02_voice_tone_style.md` for guidelines
2. Check draft against voice/tone guidelines
3. Adjust writing to match desired voice/tone
4. Ensure consistency across sections

## Output

Update `docs/<DOC>/05_draft.md` to align with voice/tone guidelines.

## Guidelines

- Voice should be consistent throughout
- Tone may vary slightly by section (e.g., more formal in some areas)
- Refer to examples in `universal/02_voice_tone_style.md`
- Ensure voice/tone matches audience expectations
