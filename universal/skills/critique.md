# Critique Skill

Critique the draft and identify gaps or areas for improvement.

## Purpose

Diagnose gaps, identify improvements, and prioritize next edits.

## Process

1. Review the complete draft
2. Check against `00_doc_goal.md` - does it achieve the goal?
3. Identify gaps or weak areas
4. Prioritize improvements
5. Document findings in `06_review_notes.md`

## Output

Update `docs/<DOC>/06_review_notes.md` with:
- Overall critique
- Prioritized edits
- Questions to resolve
- Next steps

## Guidelines

- Be specific in critique
- Prioritize edits (high/medium/low)
- Identify both strengths and weaknesses
- Provide actionable next steps
